Kaimati

Kaimati is a popular East African sweet snack made from deep-fried dough balls, soaked in a sugary syrup. Crisp on the outside, soft inside, and often flavored with cardamom.

$5.00

Viazi Karai

Viazi karai is a Kenyan swahili snack made of boiled potato slices coated in a spiced gram flour batter, then deep-fried until golden. Often served with chili or coconut chutney.

$5.00

Samaki wa Kupakwa

Samaki wa kupakwa is a Swahili dish of grilled fish slathered in a rich coconut-based sauce, flavored with spices like turmeric and garlic. It's typically served with rice or chapati.
